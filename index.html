Dr. Usama Ijaz Bajwa

Tenured Associate Professor, Department of Computer Science
COMSATS University Islamabad, Lahore Campus, Pakistan

Current Roles

  • Tenured Associate Professor, COMSATS University Islamabad, Lahore Campus.
  • Head, Machine Perception & Visual Intelligence Research Group (MPVIR).
  • Program Chair (International Conference on Frontiers of Information Technology (FIT), 2014 - 2024).
  • Director, Artificial Intelligence Education Foundation (AIEF).

Past Roles

  • Head of Product and Engineering Manager (Machine Learning), Persivia Inc. / Soliton Systems.
  • Served as an Assistant Professor and Associate Head of Department (Aug 2017-Jan 2021) at COMSATS University Islamabad, Lahore Campus (2015).
  • Served as an Assistant Professor at COMSATS University Islamabad, Abbottabad Campus (2010).
  • Visiting researcher at Medical Imaging Research Group, University of South Wales (2007).
  • Moderator/ Founder Member of Vision and Pattern Recognition Systems Research Group (VisPRS) (2008).
  • Team Lead in a research project "Automatic Personal Identification Biometrics System (APIBS) for Large Scale Applications" funded by IGNITE (National Technology Fund) formerly known as National ICT R&D Fund, Ministry of Information Technology, Pakistan (2008).
  • Co-Founded Technology Nucleus (Pvt) Ltd. (TECHNUC) (2008) and AgriAsan (2018).
  • Enrolled as a PhD student at C@SE Islamabad (2008).
  • Moderator/ Member of Machine Vision and Pattern Analysis (MVPA) Research Group at C@SE Islamabad (2005).
  • Enrolled as MS student at C@SE Islamabad (2005).
  • Served as a Lecturer and an Assistant System Analyst at COMSATS University Islamabad, Abbottabad Campus (2003).
  • Have also worked with EU election observer mission as a coordinator in the General Elections (2002).
  • Was awarded a scholarship from Ministry of Science & Technology (MoST) Pakistan and 8th in the test held for this scholarship (2000).

Research Interests

  • Video Analytics
  • Medical Image Processing
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Agri Informatics
